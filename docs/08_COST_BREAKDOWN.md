## 1. Philosophy: Start Free, Scale Smart

MASHAER JEWELLERY is designed to launch at **$0/month** using free tiers of every service. You only start paying when your business grows enough to justify the cost. Every paid upgrade has a clear trigger point.

At an average order value of **150 AED** with **~40% margin**:

- **$0/month costs** → Any sale is profit (minus payment fees)
- **$46/month costs** → ~3 orders/month to break even

## 9. Money-Saving Tips

1. **Stay on free tiers as long as possible** — Don't pre-upgrade
2. **Optimize images before upload** — Reduces Supabase Storage costs
3. **Use client-side compression** — Compress images in the browser before uploading to Supabase
4. **Cache aggressively** — Reduces serverless function invocations
5. **Lazy-load images** — Reduces bandwidth per visit
6. **Use WebP format** — 30% smaller than JPEG with same quality
7. **Monitor usage weekly** — Check Supabase and Vercel dashboards
8. **Clean up unused data** — Delete draft products and test orders regularly
